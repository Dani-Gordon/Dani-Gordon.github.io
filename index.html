About Me


Creative by nature, lover of all things cheese, problem-solving is what makes me tick and puzzles are my jam! I am motivated by impacting the community around me, have a background in Human Services and a passion for coding. I love to make things, take things apart and put them back together.

Working in the tech industry has always been a dream of mine. In 2021, I had a lighbulb moment and realized the only way to truly be happy was to change career paths and make that dream become my reality. Since then, I have enrolled in and completed the Software Engineering Immersive Course through General Assembly, am excited for what the future holds and ready to creatively solve all the problems and puzzles that come my way!

During my free time, you will find me fly fishing, camping, traveling, watching football, drinking coffee, behind the decks or on the dance floor exploring my diverse music addiction.
